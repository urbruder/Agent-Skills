# Review Mining Framework

## Signals to extract

- repeated complaints
- surprise delights
- purchase triggers
- comparison language
- product expectations
- return/refund reasons
- quality concerns
- sizing/fit issues
- packaging issues
- shipping issues
- trust concerns
- use cases
- buyer identity

## Insight types

### Product insight
What to improve, remove, add, bundle, explain, or photograph.

### Copy insight
Words, hooks, objections, and benefits customers already use.

### Positioning insight
How customers compare options and what they value most.

### Merchandising insight
How to improve images, bullets, titles, variants, bundles, FAQs, or PDP flow.

## Review evidence rules

Use short paraphrases or brief snippets. Do not over-index on one dramatic review. Rank insights by frequency and commercial impact.

## Competitor ethics

Do not plagiarize competitor copy. Use reviews to understand market gaps and customer language.
