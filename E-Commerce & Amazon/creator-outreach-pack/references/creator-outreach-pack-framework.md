# Creator Outreach Framework

## Creator types

- UGC creator
- micro-influencer
- affiliate
- ambassador
- expert reviewer
- niche educator
- lifestyle creator
- unboxing creator

## Outreach principles

- personalize the first line
- explain why the fit makes sense
- make the ask clear
- make compensation or product exchange clear
- reduce back-and-forth
- respect creative voice
- avoid mass-blast language

## Brief essentials

- product overview
- audience
- key talking points
- claims to avoid
- required shots
- forbidden claims
- deliverables
- timeline
- usage rights placeholder
- disclosure reminder

## Compliance note

Remind creators to follow applicable sponsorship disclosure rules. Do not script fake personal experiences.
