# Seasonal Campaign Framework

## Common seasonal phases

- planning
- teaser
- early access
- launch
- mid-campaign reminder
- social proof
- urgency
- last chance
- post-promo follow-up
- winback

## Offer types

- percentage discount
- dollar-off
- free shipping
- gift with purchase
- bundle
- buy more save more
- early access
- limited edition
- loyalty reward
- no-discount value add

## Seasonal angles

- giftability
- preparedness
- limited seasonal use
- self-care
- hosting
- travel
- routines
- family
- new year reset
- back-to-school
- Q4 urgency

## Metrics

Revenue, conversion rate, AOV, margin, list growth, email CTR, SMS CTR, sell-through, returns, new vs returning customers.
