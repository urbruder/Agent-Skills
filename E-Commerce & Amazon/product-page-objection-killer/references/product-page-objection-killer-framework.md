# Objection Handling Framework

## Objection categories

- price/value
- size/fit
- quality/durability
- compatibility
- ease of use
- shipping/delivery
- returns/exchanges
- safety
- ingredients/materials
- trust/company
- results/performance
- maintenance/care
- gift suitability

## Page placements

### Hero/above fold
Handle primary promise and trust.

### Bullets
Handle benefits and feature clarity.

### Images
Handle size, use, contents, before/after, material, setup.

### FAQ
Handle detailed concerns.

### Reviews/proof
Handle credibility.

### Guarantee/returns
Handle purchase risk.

## Answer formula

1. acknowledge concern
2. give clear answer
3. support with proof or policy
4. connect back to benefit
