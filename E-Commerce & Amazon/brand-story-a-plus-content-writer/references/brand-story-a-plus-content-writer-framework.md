# A+ Content Framework

## Strong A+ content does

- clarify why the product exists
- connect features to real use moments
- make the brand feel trustworthy
- reduce perceived risk
- support visual storytelling
- answer why this product over alternatives

## Module types

- brand origin
- problem and solution
- ingredient/material/quality story
- use case grid
- comparison chart
- lifestyle transformation
- founder note
- sustainability or mission, only if true
- guarantee or support, only if provided

## Tone guidance

Emotional but not melodramatic. Premium but not vague. Specific but not cluttered.

## Claim safety

Do not invent certifications, sourcing, sustainability claims, test results, or health benefits.
