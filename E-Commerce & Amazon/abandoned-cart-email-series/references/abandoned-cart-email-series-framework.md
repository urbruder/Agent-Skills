# Cart Recovery Framework

## Flow timing

Typical starting point:
- Email 1: 1-3 hours after abandonment
- Email 2: 18-24 hours later
- Email 3: 48-72 hours later

Adjust for purchase complexity and product price.

## Email roles

### Email 1
Simple reminder. Reduce friction. Show product/cart.

### Email 2
Rebuild desire. Address objections. Highlight proof, benefits, shipping, returns, or support.

### Email 3
Final nudge. Use legitimate urgency, incentive, or reassurance.

## Incentive ladder

Prefer:
1. reminder
2. benefit/proof
3. free shipping or bonus
4. small discount if margin allows

## Objections

- price
- shipping cost
- delivery time
- trust
- sizing/fit
- quality
- returns
- need versus want
- comparison shopping
