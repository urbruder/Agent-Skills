# Webinar/VSL Flow Framework

Use this reference for advanced flow design, slide mapping, and pitch structure.

## Core conversion arc

1. Pattern interrupt
2. Audience qualification
3. Promise
4. Problem diagnosis
5. Old way versus new way
6. New mechanism
7. Teaching pillars
8. Proof
9. Offer bridge
10. Offer stack
11. Risk reversal if legitimate
12. Objection handling
13. CTA

## Big idea formulas

- The reason [audience] struggle with [problem] is not [common belief], it is [new insight].
- You do not need [old requirement] to get [desired outcome]. You need [mechanism].
- Most [audience] are solving [problem] at the wrong level.

## Teaching pillar structure

Each teaching pillar should include:

- name
- mistake or misconception
- new principle
- example
- action step
- connection to the offer

## Offer stack structure

Clarify:

- core deliverable
- implementation support
- templates or assets
- community or feedback
- timeline
- expected next step
- legitimate bonuses, if provided
- guarantee or risk reducer, if provided

## Objection library

Common objections:

- I do not have time.
- This will be too expensive.
- I have tried this before.
- My business is different.
- I need to think about it.
- I do not trust myself to implement.
- I need my team or partner to approve.
- I am not ready yet.
