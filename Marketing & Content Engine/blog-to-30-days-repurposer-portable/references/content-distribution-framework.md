# Content Distribution Framework

Balance a 30-day repurposing calendar across these roles:

- Authority posts: show expertise and point of view.
- Education posts: teach a useful step, framework, or checklist.
- Problem posts: clarify pain, risk, or hidden cost.
- Proof posts: use examples, data, testimonials, or case logic when available.
- Perspective posts: explain a belief, trend, or contrarian view.
- Engagement posts: ask a useful question or invite reflection.
- Conversion posts: connect the topic to an offer, resource, or next step.

A strong 30-day calendar should not be 30 summaries. It should feel like a campaign built from one source idea.
