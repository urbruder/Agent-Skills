# Blog-to-30-Days Repurposer

Turn one long-form content asset into 30 days of useful business social posts.

## Workflow

1. Extract the source article's core ideas:
   - main argument
   - target audience
   - business problem
   - promised outcome
   - supporting points
   - examples, stats, frameworks, or stories
   - product, service, or CTA if present

2. Build a 30-day content mix using varied angles:
   - insight
   - how-to
   - mistake
   - myth
   - checklist
   - story
   - contrarian take
   - data point
   - framework
   - question
   - opinion
   - objection handling
   - case/example
   - CTA post

3. Generate ready-to-post content, not just ideas.

4. Avoid repeating the same point in slightly different words. Each day should have a distinct angle.

5. Preserve the original argument and expertise while adapting the format for social distribution.

## Platform adaptation

If the user names a platform, adapt copy accordingly:

- LinkedIn: professional, insight-led, credible, clear CTA.
- X or Threads: concise, punchy, often broken into short posts or threads.
- Instagram: caption-friendly, skimmable, visual idea included.
- Email/newsletter: stronger subject line and more context.

If no platform is specified, default to LinkedIn-style B2B posts that can be adapted elsewhere.

## Quality rules

- Do not fabricate facts, statistics, customer stories, or claims not present in the source.
- When source details are thin, use safe, clearly framed general business advice.
- Avoid generic motivational content.
- Use practical language a business audience would actually publish.
- Include CTAs that match the source: comment, save, share, read, reply, book, download, subscribe, or audit.
