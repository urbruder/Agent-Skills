# Short-Form Retention Framework

Use this reference for hook generation, retention edits, and platform-specific script strategy.

## Retention mechanics

### Open loop
Create a question the viewer wants answered.
Example: "The reason your ads are not converting is not your copy."

### Pattern interrupt
Break expectation quickly.
Example: "Your best content idea is probably buried in your sales calls."

### Specificity
Replace generic topics with concrete situations.
Weak: "How to grow your business."
Strong: "How consultants can turn one client objection into 7 posts."

### Stakes
Explain why the idea matters.
Example: "This is why your best leads disappear after the first call."

### Payoff
Deliver the promised insight before the viewer feels tricked.

## Hook categories

### Mistake hook
"Stop [action] if you want [outcome]."

### Contrarian hook
"Everyone says [common advice]. I would do the opposite."

### Cost hook
"[Problem] is costing you [business consequence]."

### Identity hook
"If you're a [audience], this is for you."

### Curiosity hook
"I changed one thing about [process], and it fixed [problem]."

### Proof hook
"We tested [thing]. Here is what happened."
Only use proof if provided.

### Myth hook
"The biggest myth about [topic] is..."

## Script pacing

### 15 seconds
1 idea, 3-4 beats, no intro.

### 30 seconds
1 idea, 5-6 beats, quick CTA.

### 60 seconds
1 idea with context, example, takeaway, CTA.

## Visual beat ideas

- talking head with jump cuts
- text overlays
- screen recording
- whiteboard
- product demo
- behind-the-scenes clip
- before/after visual
- tweet/post screenshot
- checklist overlay
- b-roll tied to metaphor

## CTA types

### Engagement
"Comment [word] and I will send it."

### Lead magnet
"Grab the free [resource]."

### Sales
"Book a call if you want help with this."

### Education
"Follow for more [topic] breakdowns."

### Soft CTA
"Save this before your next [task]."

## Retention edit checklist

- Cut the greeting.
- Move the payoff earlier.
- Replace generic advice with a specific example.
- Add a visual change every 2-4 seconds.
- Remove secondary ideas.
- Make the CTA match the viewer's current awareness.
