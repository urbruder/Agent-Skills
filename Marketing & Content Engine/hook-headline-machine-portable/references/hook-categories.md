# Hook Categories

Use these categories to create variety:

- Problem: names a costly or frustrating issue.
- Outcome: promises a credible business result.
- Contrarian: challenges a common belief.
- Curiosity: opens an information gap without clickbait.
- Authority: signals experience, data, or expert perspective.
- Mistake: points to a common error.
- Myth: corrects a false assumption.
- How-to: gives a practical path.
- Before/after: contrasts old and new ways.
- Executive insight: frames the issue at leadership level.
