# Repurposing Platform Framework

Use this reference for platform-native adaptation and weekly content planning.

## Atomization methods

### Slice by angle
Turn one idea into:
- pain point
- myth
- how-to
- story
- proof
- checklist
- objection
- prediction
- behind-the-scenes
- CTA post

### Slice by audience awareness
- unaware: educate around the problem
- problem-aware: name the pain and stakes
- solution-aware: compare approaches
- product-aware: show differentiator and proof
- most-aware: give direct offer or CTA

### Slice by format
- text post
- carousel
- thread
- short video
- email
- poll
- quote
- checklist

## Default 7-platform roles

### LinkedIn
Authority and business relevance.

### X
Sharp insight and fast reach.

### Instagram
Visual education and saves.

### Threads
Conversational connection.

### TikTok/Reels
Discovery and attention.

### YouTube Shorts
Searchable short-form discovery.

### Email
Depth, trust, and conversion.

## CTA matching

### Awareness CTA
- Follow for more
- Save this
- Share with your team

### Engagement CTA
- Comment with your biggest blocker
- Which one would you try first?

### Lead CTA
- Download the guide
- DM me the word [keyword]
- Join the list

### Sales CTA
- Book a call
- Request a demo
- See the offer

## Weekly publishing order

A strong weekly sequence:

1. LinkedIn: authority post
2. X: short take or thread
3. Reels/TikTok: discovery hook
4. Instagram carousel: save-worthy breakdown
5. Threads: conversational take
6. YouTube Shorts: concise explainer
7. Email: deeper story and CTA

## Quality checklist

Every repurposed piece should answer:

- Why should this audience care here?
- What is the native format?
- What is the one idea?
- What action should happen next?
- Does this feel adapted, not copied?
